Anuradhapura Kingdom

437 BCE ‚Äì 1017 CE

First major kingdom; irrigation & Buddhist center

The Anuradhapura Kingdom was the first major kingdom of Sri Lanka and served as the island's capital for over 1,400 years. It was the cradle of Sinhalese Buddhist civilization and became one of the most important centers of Buddhism in the ancient world. The kingdom was renowned for its sophisticated irrigation systems and magnificent stupas.
